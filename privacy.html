Privacy Policy – Comic Vault App

Effective date: 2025-10-19

Comic Vault App stores your catalog and invoices locally on your device. The app does not create accounts and does not collect personal data for analytics or advertising.

Data we handle

  • Inventory and invoice data you enter is saved on your device.
  • File access is used only to export or share invoice files you request.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Permissions

  • Storage/Media: to read or write invoice files you choose to share.

Third-party SDKs

No advertising SDKs. No analytics SDKs.

Security

Files shared from the app use temporary read-only links via Android FileProvider.

Contact

Email: support@comicvault.app

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will appear on this page with a new effective date.
